What is yown?

yown (short for you own) is an android app to help you declutter and keep track of your inventory.

What features are supported?

  • NFC functionality for updating items
  • A SQLite DB for persistent storage
  • Suggestions for what to keep and what to let go

How do I reset the app?

You can reset all entries in the app in the settings activity. Choose Settings > RESET ALL DATA

Do I need to have NFC enabled?

No, it's not necessary to have NFC enabled. But I highly recommend it, as it will make decluttering much easier. You can enable NFC in Android > Settings > NFC

How do I update existing items?

You can update items by clicking on them in the Main screen.

How do I update the NFC tag

Updating the NFC tag is not possible. The relationship between an item and the NFC tag is lifelong. If you want to update the NFC tag just delete the existing item and create a new one.
